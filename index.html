Pauline Lomba

Hello, my name is Pauline Lomba. I am a passion-driven astrophysicist.

Having just graduated with a Master’s Degree in Astrophysics, Space Sciences, and Planetology from Toulouse III Paul Sabatier University in France, my life's goal is to contribute to the advancement of astrophysics. I am particularly passionate about planetary and exoplanetary sciences, as well as exobiology—an emerging discipline at the intersection of worlds that seeks to answer two essential questions: how does prebiotic chemistry create life, and are we alone in the universe? To pursue this goal and complete my educational background, I am currently doing additional training in biology, biochemistry, and microbiology, with the aim of finding a PhD program in planetary science, exoplanetary science, or exobiology by fall 2025.
